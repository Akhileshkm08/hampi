History of Hampi

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the glorious history of a bygone era.

Virupaksha Temple

The Virupaksha Temple is believed to have been originally established in the 7th century during the Chalukya dynasty, with references to it found in ancient texts and inscriptions. The earliest structure in the temple complex was built by the Chalukya King Vikramaditya II (c. 733-744 CE) to honor Lord Shiva in his form as Virupaksha.

Stone Chariot

A masterpiece of craftsmanship, the Stone Chariot in the Vittala Temple complex symbolizes the artistic excellence of the Vijayanagara Empire. Its detailed carvings make it a must-see for history enthusiasts.

Lotus Mahal

A blend of Hindu and Islamic architectural styles, the Lotus Mahal resembles a blooming lotus and was designed as a leisure palace for the royal family.
